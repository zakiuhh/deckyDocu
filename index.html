Autonomous Aerial Dominance Redefined

The DECKY CAP Drone fuses technologies from sixth-generation jets, hypersonic propulsion, AI-driven swarm warfare, and adaptive stealth into one integrated system-of-systems - redefining what drones are capable of.

What is DECKY CAP?

The future of aerial warfare is shifting towards autonomy, speed, and smart swarm integration. Traditional distinctions between drones, fighters, and missiles are blurring. The DECKY CAP Drone positions itself at this convergence - capable of engaging targets across multiple domains, coordinating with manned assets, and dynamically adapting to rapidly evolving threat environments.

XH-800 Hydra Hypersonic Engine
A hybrid multi-mode propulsion system operating from subsonic takeoff all the way to Mach 8 and exo-atmospheric conditions.
System Overview

The XH-800 integrates turbofan, ramjet, rotating detonation engine (RDRE), scramjet, and rocket technologies into a single unified propulsion architecture.

Stage Breakdown

Stage Technology Function

Mach 1 – 3 Ramjet Efficient mid-speed thrust
Mach 3 – 8 RDRE + Scramjet Continuous hypersonic flight
Space-Transition Rocket Augmenter Exo-atmospheric capability
